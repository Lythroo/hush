# Hush

Quiets harsh Minecraft sounds — like the enderman scream and the nether portal — and fades a
sound out when its source dies or despawns. Client-side.

Built from one shared codebase for **Fabric** and **NeoForge** across Minecraft **1.21.1**,
**1.21.11**, **26.1** and **26.2**.

## Settings

Open the config screen (NeoForge: Mods list → Hush → Config; Fabric: Mod Menu, or bind a key
under Controls → Hush). You get a master toggle, "silence dying entities", de-spatialise the
enderman, tame the portal sound, and the fade-out duration and curve.
