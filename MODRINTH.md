# Hush

**Quiets Minecraft's harshest sounds, and fades them out when their source is gone.**

A lightweight, client-side mod that tames the few sounds that are too loud or never stop, without muting the game.

## Features

- **Softens the enderman** - quiets the scream and de-spatialises the "stare" drone (toggle: stereo or 3D).
- **Fades dying sounds** - a mob's lingering sounds fade out when it dies or despawns (death sounds kept).
- **Tames the nether portal** - the portal sound fades when you step away.
- **Tunable fade** - adjust the fade-out duration and curve.
- **Clean in-game config** - one simple settings page, no walls of options.

## Compatibility

- **Client-side only** - works on any server, including vanilla.
- **Fabric & NeoForge** · Minecraft **1.21.1**, **1.21.11**, **26.1**, **26.2**.
- **Fabric API** required on Fabric. **Mod Menu** optional (never bundled).
